Why us?

Furthermore, sustainable practices can inspire and empower individuals to become agents of change, creating a ripple effect of positive impact on a global scale. It is time for consumers to recognize the immense power they possess and take responsibility for the impact they have on the planet. Solutions Offerings

After taking into consideration factors such as the amount of water needed, space restrictions, and roof square footage, we use sustainable practices to build the custom rain collection system. Based on the scenario, we re-use old materials like garbage cans or flower pots to reduce waste and build the custom rain water storage tank. We ensure that the collected water is stored safely and easily accessible. We will also hook up the rain water collection system to any irrigation systems in place to provide water to your garden.

Rain Reap

By collecting rain water, homeowners can reduce their water bills and provide more natural water to their plants. Unlike tap or city water which is littered with minerals, salts, and other treatment chemicals, rain water is clean, natural, and relatively pure. Our service Rain Reap aims to provide homeowners with a consultation of their rain water needs and use engineering to implement a smart and efficient rain water collection system.

IoT Devices

Believe it or not, homes are getting smarter by the day. IoT devices, or Internet of Things devices, are a transformative technology that are changing the way we interact with the world around us. Simply put, IoT devices are everyday objects that are connected to the internet and can communicate with each other and with us. Now, why are these devices so useful? Well, IoT devices provide us with a wealth of data and insights that were previously unavailable to us. By collecting and analyzing data from these devices, we can gain a deeper understanding of how things work and how we can optimize them for greater efficiency and productivity. We aim to build affordable and easy to use IoT devices for the average consumer to improve the sustainability of their homes and make their lives better.

We aim to provide affordable and easy to use IoT devices for the average consumer to improve the sustainability of their homes and make their lives better. For example, imagine a smart thermostat that you can control from your phone and uses AI to learn your behavior patterns and adjusts the temperature in your home automatically to save energy.
